About me

Currently I'm in graduate school at Arizona State University pursuing a Ph.D. in Media Arts and Sciences. My research focuses on developing interactive visual media for stroke rehabilitation, with a focus on retraining upper extremity movement of stroke survivors. Before grad school, I attended Tulane University from 2003-2008 to study painting and biomedical engineering.

My future research and development interests lie in mobile apps for creativity. I'm a big fan of Paper by 53 and Figure by Propellerhead.
