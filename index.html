I'm pursuing a Ph.D. in Media Arts and Sciences in the School of Arts, Media and Engineering at Arizona State University. My research focuses on developing interactive visual media for motor rehabilitation, with a focus on retraining upper extremity movement of stroke survivors. Before ASU, I attended Tulane University from 2003-2008 to study painting and biomedical engineering. I'm currently interested in mobile apps for health and activity awareness.
